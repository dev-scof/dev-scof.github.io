Project Portfolio

SE.SCO

Project

As the years go by, agricultural damage caused by plant pests is increasing, but many pests are not even counted.

In particular, criticism is raised that the lack of experts is a factor that domestic agriculture is helplessly affected by the attack on pests. Amid the discovery of foreign pests one after another due to climate change, the reality is that there is a lack of experts who can find the first foreign pests on time and present a clear control method.

As such, the number of pests of foreign species is increasing and the damage is increasing, but the reality is that there is an infinite lack of experts who can respond to this.

Therefore, this project presents an AI-based pest detection system solution that can technically solve and respond quickly to problems with existing shortage of experts.

COMTRIS

Project

Unlike in the past, most people use prefabricated computers rather than finished computers these days.

Each user is free to customize quotes for special purposes.

However, users who do not have knowledge of computer hardware have a lot of difficulty fitting assembled computers.

Also, there are people who only know representative hardware and lack knowledge of other additional devices.

Comtris is a system that targets these users and recommends estimates for assembled PCs.

For example, 
- I think the CPU needs Ryzen 5600 to run a particular program, but what other parts should I use?
- I want RAM's graphics card to be RTX3060, but what should I use for other parts?

If you look at many people's estimates of assembled PCs, you'll find one feature. Certain CPUs use certain M/B and GPUs a lot. (Of course, due to the compatibility of the CPU's sockets, there are differences in these areas because they are the same M/B chipset, but their detailed performance varies from non-reference to non-reference.)

Therefore, it is a system that uses these features to learn six parts (CPU/GPU/RAM/M.B/SSD/POWER) with indicators such as non-reference (brand), performance, and specifications to recommend these other parts to the user.

Indecisive

Project

There are many good restaurants near Sejong University, so students always think about what to eat when it's meal time. The "Indecisive" project was created to shorten the time to think about what to eat.

"Indecisive" recommends food (restaurants) that can be eaten near Sejong University for Sejong University students who are thinking about what to eat whenever it is meal time.

To consider user preferences, we have created categories of "Korean food," "Western food," "Chinese food," and "Japanese food," which allow user to determine restaurants in specific categories.

When a restaurant is recommended, it shows information about the restaurant and representative menus.

In addition, we have added a "drawing lots" game that users can easily enjoy.
